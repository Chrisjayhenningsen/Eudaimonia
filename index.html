What this is

Stop seeing ads for things you're trying to quit. Eudaimonia replaces distracting ads with recommendations actually aligned with your goals.

You tell Eudaimonia what you're working towards — getting fit, reading more, building better habits — and what gets in your way. Then right-click any ad that's pulling you in the wrong direction and block it. Check in weekly on your progress and earn tokens. Where the ads were, you'll see community-recommended products matched to your actual goals instead.

Every ad system optimizes for clicks, not for your wellbeing. Eudaimonia flips the model: the recommendations you see are paid for by people who genuinely believe their product belongs in your life — and filtered by what you said you want.

Who it's for: people who are actively working on themselves and tired of the internet working against them — and people with a product or service genuinely worth sharing with the right customer.

The token economy

Our pricing is based on the median cost-per-click across advertising markets — roughly $0.25 per token. As that median rises with inflation, so will ours.

Tokens are earned free through weekly check-ins. Advertisers spend tokens to promote their links — each click costs one token from their budget.

Because our users declare their goals upfront, your ad reaches someone far more likely to convert than a cold impression. Better targeting, transparent pricing, no middlemen extracting rent.
