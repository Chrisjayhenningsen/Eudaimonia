PRIVACY POLICY - EUDAIMONIA EXTENSION
Last Updated: February 20, 2026
Introduction
Eudaimonia ("we," "our," or "us") is a Chrome extension that helps you block distracting advertisements and discover products aligned with your personal development goals. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.
Information We Collect
Personal Development Data (Stored Locally)

Your personal goals, obstacles, daily habits, and product preferences
Weekly check-in responses about your progress
Token balance and invite count

This information is stored only in your browser's local storage using Chrome's sync storage. We never send this data to our servers.
Anonymous User Identification

When you first use Eudaimonia, we generate an anonymous user ID using Firebase Authentication
This ID cannot be linked to your identity and is only used to track which promotions you've submitted
No email, name, or other personally identifiable information is collected

Community Data (Stored in Firebase)

URLs, titles, descriptions, and keywords of products you choose to promote to other users
Number of clicks your promoted links receive
Aggregated, anonymous keyword statistics from all users' goals (e.g., "productivity: 47 users")

Ad Blocking Data (Stored Locally)

Sources and categories of advertisements you choose to block
This data remains only on your device

How We Use Your Information

Locally stored goals: To match you with relevant community recommendations
Anonymous user ID: To track which promotions you submitted and show you click statistics
Promoted links: To display recommendations to other users with matching goals
Aggregated keywords: To help promoters understand what users are seeking (shown anonymously, e.g., "fitness: 23 users")

Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. Ever.
Third-Party Services We Use:

Firebase (Google): Stores community promotions and anonymous aggregated statistics. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
Future payment processing (when implemented): We will use services like Stripe or PayPal to process token purchases. We will never see or store your credit card information. Only transaction confirmations (amount paid, date) will be recorded.

Your Data Rights

Personal goals and habits: Stored only on your device. Delete the extension to remove all local data
Promoted links: Contact us to request removal of your submissions
Anonymous ID: Cannot be linked to you, but you can request data deletion by contacting us

Data Security

Local data is protected by Chrome's built-in storage encryption
Firebase data is transmitted over HTTPS
We use Firebase security rules to prevent unauthorized access
No passwords or authentication credentials are ever stored (anonymous authentication only)

Children's Privacy
Eudaimonia is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
Future Payment Features
When we introduce the ability to purchase tokens:

Payments will be processed by third-party services (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
We will never see or store your credit card information
Only transaction confirmations will be recorded
You'll receive receipts via email (if provided)

Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy as we add features. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
